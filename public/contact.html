Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

❓ How do I create a note?

Simply click on the "Add Note" button in the navigation menu, fill in your note title and content, then click "Save Note".

❓ Is my data secure?

Yes! All your notes are securely stored in our MongoDB database with proper encryption and security measures.

❓ Can I edit or delete my notes?

Absolutely! Each note has edit and delete options available when you view your notes list.

❓ Is NoteHub free to use?

Yes, NoteHub is completely free to use for all users. No hidden charges or subscriptions required.
